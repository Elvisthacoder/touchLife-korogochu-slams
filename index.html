About

It has its genesis in one Catherine Njumba who was inspired to start a daycare center in Korogocho slums, Nairobi in 2008. Earlier on a tragic incident had happened when 4 children succumbed to a fire when they were left home alone. Catherine was approached by several mothers, requesting her to care for their children when they go to work. Touchlife is a primary school and has a vision to become a childrenâ€™s home that will offer basic needs to these vulnerable children. Most of these children are orphans, single-parented more so amongst them are HIV positive. The center has been growing at a slow pace and managed to reach standard 5 this year, hoping to reach level 8. It has a current population of 74 children ranging between 4-12 years.
